Orange Endive Salad

Description

a original salad which mix sweet and salted flavour.

This recipe is part of my family inheritance.

Ingredients

  • 1 to 4 oranges
  • 4 endives
  • a bunch of wallnuts
  • balsamic vinegar
  • colza or oliva oil (you can even mix the two of them)
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Gently wash the endives and dry them with a clean cloth.
  2. Peel oranges and cut into pieces into a large bowl
  3. cut endives into pieces and add them to the bowl
  4. Broke the nuts and add them.
  5. Make the salad dressing
  6. Remove gently with a two spoon and serve.
